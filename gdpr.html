Legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects

There are some legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects the main one being GDPR - General Data Protection Regulation. GDPR is a law that was brought in to ensure that any companies dealing with data are held responsible for that data. It sets guidelines as to what can and can’t be done with personal data. If a company loses, steals, steals, or changes someone’s personal information then they will be held accountable for it and left with a hefty fine. GDPR can affect digital projects, companies need to be compliant with GDPR. Let’s take website projects as an example - coding decisions and designs have to be made to keep data collection legal and to a minimum and then that data has to be strongly protected with decent security systems.[5]
